About Our Company

Our story

A family business built on trust

Wake Heating and Air is family-owned and operated right here in Raleigh, NC. As native North Carolinians, we understand how unpredictable and extreme the weather can be in our state — that’s why we guarantee service within 24 hours.

Personalized service that best suits your residential HVAC needs is our specialty. Unlike some of our national competitors, our technicians are highly trained heating and air specialists, not salesmen in a service technician’s uniform.

We realize that the key to our success is earning your trust by offering exceptional service on every job, every time, guaranteed.
